Boyu Fan

About me

I'm a PhD candidate in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at MIT, where I work with Professors Triantaphyllos R. Akylas and Thomas Peacock. I am funded as a NSF Graduate Research Fellow.

Prior to MIT, I was an undergrad at Caltech, where I worked with Mory Gharib and Julia R. Greer.

Research interests

I'm interested in a range of problems relating to the modelling of fluid systems, especially ones of the nonlinear flavor. Currently, I've been exploring various instabilities of internal gravity waves and their interaction with mean flows.
